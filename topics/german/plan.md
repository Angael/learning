# Topic plan: German

## End goal

Angeal can handle practical everyday German conversations and build durable vocabulary/grammar through spaced retrieval.

## Progression

1. Pronunciation and survival phrases.
2. Present tense sentence skeletons.
3. Cases as role markers.
4. Everyday topics: food, travel, work, small talk.
5. Listening/reading practice with graded material.
6. Short original conversations.

## Achievements

- [ ] Introduce yourself in German.
- [ ] Order food politely.
- [ ] Explain what nominative/accusative are for.
- [ ] Hold a 2-minute guided dialogue.
