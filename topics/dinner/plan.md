# Topic plan: Dinner

## End goal

Angeal can plan and cook satisfying dinners by understanding constraints: time, ingredients, technique, nutrition, cleanup, and taste balance.

## Progression

1. Dinner as constraints: protein, vegetables, starch, sauce, time.
2. Knife/prep and mise en place.
3. Heat transfer: pan, oven, boiling, steaming.
4. Flavor balance: salt, acid, fat, sweetness, heat.
5. Meal planning and leftovers.
6. Improvisation from pantry ingredients.

## Achievements

- [ ] Plan a 30-minute dinner from available ingredients.
- [ ] Explain why salt timing matters.
- [ ] Build a simple sauce.
- [ ] Reuse leftovers intentionally.
